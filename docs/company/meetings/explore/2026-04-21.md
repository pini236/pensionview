## Suggested quarter theme

> **"Know your pension, earn its trust"**

Two reinforcing arcs:
- **Trust the product** — regulatory disclosure, privacy policy, pipeline reliability, extraction confidence, instrumentation. Answers "is this product serious?"
- **Know your pension** — goal activation, investment track alert, niud radar, keren-hishtalmut ceiling. Answers "does this tell me anything I couldn't figure out myself?"

The theme is deliberately not "build more features" — it's establish trust + tell Israelis the truth about their pension, then let signal quality generate word-of-mouth.
